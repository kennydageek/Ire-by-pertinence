Welcome to Ire

Ire by Pertinence is your best destination for work and play. IRE boasts of a serene environment powered by nature, built around technology, and designed with people in mind. FAQS

We have compiled most frequent questions asked and our answers to them

1. Where is IRE located?

Ire is a budding new smart city development located in Mowe, Ogun State, and developed by Pertinence Group. The focus is on creating an enabling, serene, and secure community for its users.

2. How can I invest in IRE?

See the project. Call a realtor or Call an in-house representative or we reach out based on the details submitted. Go for a site inspection (or not). Fill out a short form with the necessary information. Pay. Receive necessary documents.

3. What are IRE property documents?

The title document is Global C of O. Upon initial deposit, you will receive Receipts and Contracts of Sale.

4. What plot sizes are available?

300 SQM & 500 SQM are available.

5. Is there any limit to commencing construction on my land after application?

Construction commences immediately after the building plan has been approved by the estate technical team and Govt regulatory agencies.
